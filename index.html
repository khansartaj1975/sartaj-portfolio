About Me

Mr. Sartaj Khan is an experienced and dedicated academician with a solid background in Computer Science and Engineering. He currently serves as the Asst Professor and CoD of the Computer Science and Engineering Department at Shivalik College of Engineering. Mr. Sartaj Khan served as the HOD (Head of the Department) from 2006 to 2011 and has contributed significantly to various administrative roles at AKTU University. His dedication to teaching earned him the “Vishisht Shikshak Samman” award in 2008 and 2009 from the Sharda Group of Institutions for his outstanding performance as a faculty member. An alumnus of Government Engineering College, Jabalpur (MP), Mr. Khan holds an M. Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from U.P. Technical University, Lucknow. After gaining valuable industry experience for about one and a half years, Mr. Khan transitioned to academics in September 2000 by joining the Sharda Group of Institutions. He later moved to the Hindustan Institute of Management & Computer Studies, Farah, Mathura, and then to the Hindustan Institute of Technology & Management, Agra in February 2014. Mr. Khan is currently pursuing his PhD in Computer Science from Sharda University, Greater Noida. Mr. Sartaj Khan holds memberships in several prestigious professional bodies, including CSI: F80045, IAENG: 331860, IEEE: 93919558, ISTE: LM – 136500, CSTA: 198358013508, and SciencePG: 10604333. In addition, Mr. Khan is a certified B & C Level National Cadet Corps holder. His passion for teaching, active involvement in departmental activities, and student development continue to inspire learners at Shivalik College.
